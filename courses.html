Why Quick Sort

The algorithm ensures that no course is skipped or left out while sorting the list quickly. Each course is compared based on urgency, so the final list reflects the correct priority order. No matter how many courses there are, the algorithm will sort them in a predictable and efficient way. Courses with the same urgency maintain their relative order.
