1).Water Management

i). Water Distribution

Algorithm: Minimum Spanning Tree (Kruskal’s Algorithm)

Use Case: Design cost-effective water distribution systems that minimize the infrastructure cost while maintaining connectivity.

ii). Sanitation and Wastewater System

Algorithm: Shortest Path Algorithm (Dijkstra’s Algorithm)

Use Case: Optimize the transportation of wastewater from households to treatment plants to reduce contamination and ensure timely processing.

iii). Leak Detection in Water Pipes

Algorithm: Breadth-First Search (BFS)

Use Case: Detect leaks or failures in water pipes by traversing the network to identify disconnected components.

2).Transportation Infrastructure

1. Public Transport Network Design

Algorithm: Minimum Spanning Tree (Prim’s or Kruskal’s Algorithm)

Use Case: Design cost-efficient public transport routes (bus/train networks).

2. Emergency Response Routes

Algorithm: Breadth-First Search (BFS)

Use Case: Quickly determine the shortest paths for emergency vehicles.

3).Energy and Utilities

1. Energy Distribution Network Optimization

Algorithm: Minimum Spanning Tree (Kruskal’s Algorithm)

Use Case: Minimize the cost of connecting renewable energy sources (solar farms, wind turbines) to the grid.

2. Energy Storage and Distribution

Algorithm: Shortest Path Algorithm (Dijkstra’s Algorithm)

Use Case: Optimize the routing of stored energy from battery systems to high-demand areas.
