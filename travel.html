Why Boyer-Moore?

The Boyer-Moore algorithm is highly efficient compared to other string search algorithms. Its efficiency primarily comes from the fact that it skips over sections of the text that don't match the pattern, reducing the number of comparisons and making it faster than brute-force search algorithms
