Introduction

This course focuses on the design and analysis of algorithms and their real-world applications. The objective is to develop the capability to write algorithms for specific tasks, implement them using suitable data structures, and improve their efficiency. This course bridges theoretical knowledge and practical applications, making it crucial for solving computational problems.

Business Use Cases

  • Smart city infrastructure planning and development.
  • Efficient resource allocation and management using algorithms.
  • Data analysis for predictive maintenance and future planning.

Course Reflections

The course on algorithm design provided us with a strong foundation in writing and optimizing algorithms. It allowed us to bridge theoretical knowledge with practical applications, which has been instrumental in building this project.
